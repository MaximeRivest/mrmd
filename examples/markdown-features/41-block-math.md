# Block Math (LaTeX)

Block math uses double dollar signs for centered equations.

## Summation

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i = x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_n
$$

## Integration

$$
\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$$

## Quadratic Formula

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

## Matrix

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}
\times
\begin{bmatrix}
e \\
f
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
ae + bf \\
ce + df
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## Limit

$$
\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{1}{x} = 0
$$

## Multi-line Equations

$$
\begin{align}
f(x) &= x^2 + 2x + 1 \\
     &= (x + 1)^2
\end{align}
$$

## Probability

$$
P(A|B) = \frac{P(B|A) \cdot P(A)}{P(B)}
$$
