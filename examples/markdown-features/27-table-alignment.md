# Table Alignment

Column alignment is controlled with colons in the separator row.

## Left Aligned (Default)

| Left      | Left      | Left      |
|:----------|:----------|:----------|
| Text      | Text      | Text      |
| More      | More      | More      |

## Right Aligned

| Right     | Right     | Right     |
|----------:|----------:|----------:|
| Text      | Text      | Text      |
| Numbers   | 123       | 45678     |

## Center Aligned

| Center    | Center    | Center    |
|:---------:|:---------:|:---------:|
| Text      | Text      | Text      |
| Centered  | Data      | Here      |

## Mixed Alignment

| Left Aligned | Center Aligned | Right Aligned |
|:-------------|:--------------:|--------------:|
| Left         | Center         | Right         |
| Text         | Text           | 123           |
| More         | More           | 456           |
| Data         | Data           | 789           |
