# Horizontal Rules

Horizontal rules (thematic breaks) can be created in several ways.

## Three Hyphens

Content above the line.

---

Content below the line.

## Three Asterisks

Content above.

***

Content below.

## Three Underscores

Content above.

___

Content below.

## More Than Three

These also work:

----

*****

___________

## With Spaces

- - -

* * *

_ _ _
